## English

### Full Description

YPT++ is a minimalist study app designed to help you plan, focus, review, and reflect in one place.

Use the focus timer to start deep work quickly, then switch into planning, flashcards, notes, and review without losing context. Your study data stays on your device, so you can keep using the app with the same interface across iPhone, Android, and the web.

If you want AI help, YPT++ includes a built-in Lerna AI side panel. You bring your own Gemini API key, and the app sends requests directly from your device. That means you keep control over when AI is used and what content is shared.

Key features:

- Focus timer for structured study sessions
- Planner views for keeping track of goals and schedules
- Flashcards and notes for review and recall
- OCR support for turning captured questions into study prompts
- Accent color personalization
- Installable PWA plus iOS and Android packaging

Important notes:

- Voice input support is limited on iOS WebView and may require Safari for the first release.
- Reinstalling the app can clear local data until a future backup/sync layer is added.

### App Review Notes

- Users provide their own Gemini API key.
- The app does not proxy AI traffic through a developer-owned server.
- The app does not collect analytics or advertising data.

### 完整描述

YPT++ 是一款極簡學習 App，讓你在同一個介面內完成規劃、專注、複習與反思。

你可以先用專注計時器快速開始深度學習，再切換到計畫、單字卡、筆記與回顧，不需要在多個工具之間來回跳轉。你的學習資料會保留在本機裝置中，因此可以用相同的操作體驗在 iPhone、Android 與網頁版本之間使用。

如果你需要 AI 協助，YPT++ 內建 Lerna AI 側欄。你自行提供 Gemini API key，所有請求會直接從你的裝置送出，讓你能掌握何時使用 AI，以及哪些內容會被分享給第三方服務。

主要功能：

- 用於讀書節奏管理的專注計時器
- 協助安排目標與行程的計畫視圖
- 用於複習與回想的單字卡與筆記
- 可把題目照片轉成學習提示的 OCR
- 可自訂的主題 accent 色
- 可安裝 PWA 與 iOS / Android App 包裝

重要說明：

- iOS WebView 對語音輸入支援有限，第一版可能需要 Safari 才能完整使用部分語音功能。
